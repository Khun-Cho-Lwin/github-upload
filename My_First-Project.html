Simple Ways to Live Happily

Do What You Love

You'll find that when you're doing what you love,you're filled with joy. Eg-playing soccer, writing poems or watching TV.

Help Others

When we volunteer or help others,it feels good to just be of service to someone else.3993 The impact we make feels fulfilling and is a big potential source for our own happiness.

Share With Others

When we share our thoughts, our time,and our abilities with others we feel better for it. A life without sharing can become lonely.

Spend Time With Your Loved ones

People love spending time with their friends and family for good conversation, bonding and some laughs. Life's too short to live it completely alone.

Dump Negative Thinking

Become more aware of it and try replacing your negative thoughts with some positive ones. Spend less time with negative people and more time with positive people.

Be Yourself

As Steve Jobs said,'Your time is limited, so don't waste it living someone else's life. 'Accept who you are ,just be yourself, and you'll feel a world of difference.
